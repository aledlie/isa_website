## Program Details

### GovAI Summer Fellowship

- **Organization:** Centre for the Governance of AI (Oxford)
- **Duration:** Summer (typically 8-10 weeks)
- **Focus:** Applied track for AI governance research
- **Ideal for:** Candidates interested in technical AI policy research
- **Link:** [governance.ai](https://www.governance.ai/post/summer-fellowship-2026-applied-track)

### IAPS AI Policy Fellowship

- **Organization:** Institute for AI Policy and Strategy
- **Duration:** 3 months
- **Compensation:** $15,000 stipend
- **Focus:** AI policy development and research
- **Ideal for:** Legal professionals transitioning to AI policy
- **Link:** [iaps.ai](https://governance.ai/)

### OpenAI Residency

- **Organization:** OpenAI
- **Duration:** 6 months
- **Compensation:** $18,300/month (~$110,000 annualized)
- **Focus:** AI research (primarily technical, but policy roles exist)
- **Ideal for:** Candidates wanting deep AI company experience
- **Link:** [openai.com/residency](https://openai.com/residency/)

### Microsoft CELA Internship

- **Organization:** Microsoft Corporate, External, and Legal Affairs
- **Duration:** 12 weeks
- **Focus:** Placement in specific practice groups; cutting-edge technology legal issues
- **Ideal for:** Law students seeking big tech legal experience
- **Link:** [Microsoft CELA](https://careers.microsoft.com/v2/global/en/celainternship)

### Future of Privacy Forum Internships

- **Organization:** Future of Privacy Forum
- **Duration:** Varies
- **Focus:** Legal, policy, and technical work on AI, privacy, and emerging technology
- **Ideal for:** Candidates interested in privacy-AI intersection
- **Link:** [fpf.org](https://fpf.org/us-policy-internships/)
