Effective deliverables showcase both legal expertise and the ability to work across disciplines.

## 7.1 Written Deliverables

**Legal Analysis Documents**
- Regulatory compliance memos
- Policy analysis papers
- Comparative law research
- Risk assessment reports

**Business-Oriented Documents**
- Executive summaries for leadership
- Implementation roadmaps
- Training materials
- Process documentation

**Best Practices for Legal Writing Samples**
- Have both persuasive and objective writing samples ready
- Open memoranda (with original research) are preferred by employers
- Use Context-Action-Result (CAR) framework to show impact
- Quantify outcomes where possible (e.g., "reducing case preparation time by 30%")

## 7.2 Presentation Deliverables

**Stakeholder Presentations**
- Board-level compliance briefings
- Cross-functional project updates
- Final capstone presentations
- Training and education sessions

**Visual Communications**
- Compliance frameworks and flowcharts
- Risk classification matrices
- Process maps and decision trees
- Timeline and milestone visualizations

## 7.3 Technical Deliverables

**Policy and Governance Artifacts**
- AI ethics guidelines
- Compliance checklists and templates
- Standard operating procedures
- Audit frameworks

**Product Contributions**
- Feature requirements from legal perspective
- User experience feedback
- Documentation improvements
- Test case development

## 7.4 Portfolio Building

**Elements to Include**
- Writing samples demonstrating legal analysis
- Project summaries showing cross-functional collaboration
- Presentations highlighting communication skills
- Metrics demonstrating business impact

**Showcase Hybrid Skills Through**
- AI-assisted case analysis (e.g., "reducing processing time through AI-driven tools")
- Digital literacy with legal technology platforms
- Cross-disciplinary collaboration examples
- Translation of technical concepts to legal frameworks
